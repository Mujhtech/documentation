Welcome to Swerv's API documentation page! Here, we provide comprehensive information for developers to seamlessly integrate Swerv's robust financial services into their applications. Our APls allow your platform to leverage our API for issuing USD cards, enabling multicurrency wallets and facilitating transactions in the Nigerian Naira, USD, GBP, EUR, and digital currencies (Only Naira available currently). This documentation provides a unique opportunity to tap into our globally accepted payment systems, solve cross-border payment challenges, and bridge the local and international financial ecosystems. Get started with us today and innovate with our cutting-edge technology to offer your users secure, convenient, and efficient transactions.
